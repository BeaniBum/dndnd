What is Dungeons and Dragons?

Dungeons and Dragons is a table-top roleplaying game where players act as characters while one player who is called the Dungeon Master (DM) runs the game and world for the player. The goal of D&D is for you and your friends to weave a fun and epic tale together as you go through a variety of challenges that the DM puts in front of you. Whether it be finding a tomb, slaying a dragon, or convincing an orc to not raid your village D&D has no limit to the type of story you can tell.
