What Is a Dungeon Master

The Dungeon Master is the person who runs the game.

They are the non-player characters and monsters. They devise the traps, hand out treasure, and plot the story.

The DM is the most important part of Dungeons and Dragons, without the DM the players could not play D&D.

Tips For Dming

  1. Learn to think on your feet. Players will constantly do things you won't expect so it's good to learn to improvise.

  2. Prepare enough before your session. Just because you can't predict what your players will do doesn't mean you shouldn't prepare. Preparation is the key to a sussesful session.

  3. Talk to your players. Honestly communication will help you so much and it's important that you learn this sooner than later because it'll help avoid a lot of conflicts.

  4. Don't abuse your power. Just because you are the DM doesn't mean you are perfect, if you make a mistake own up to it, be fair and don't mock your players. players can't play without a DM, but you can't DM without players you need each other.
